# Add a Holding

Open the stock account detail page, tap **＋** in the top-right corner, then enter the ticker symbol, quantity, and price per share to create a position.

1. Open the stock account detail page and tap **＋** in the top-right corner.<br>
2. Enter the ticker symbol (e.g. 2330) and stock name (e.g. TSMC).<br>
3. Enter the quantity and price per share.<br>
4. Tap **Save** and the stock will appear in your holdings list.
