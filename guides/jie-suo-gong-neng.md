# Unlock the Feature

There are two ways to use Stock Asset Management:

#### 1. Purchase

Tap the **Purchase** button to make a one-time payment and unlock permanent access.

#### 2. Watch a Rewarded Ad (Limited Free Trial)

Tap **Watch Rewarded Ad** and watch the full ad to receive a few days of access.

#### Check Current Status

Go to **Settings > Stock Asset Management** to view your current unlock status and remaining days.
